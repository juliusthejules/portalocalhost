Welcome to Portalocalhost!

This project aims to provide a comprehensive cybersecurity solution for developers and enthusiasts who require a secure environment for web development and testing.

Portalocalhost offers robust network configurations, advanced privacy, and a seamless integration process to ensure that your development work remains private and secure.

Simply keep the app open or keep the web page open for ultimate online anonymity and remain untraceable!

Features:

  • Network Configurations Masked as Localhost Addresses.
  • MAC & Bluetooth Addresses set to all Zeros.
  • Hostname set to IPv4 Localhost Address.
  • DNS Settings Masked as Localhost Addresses.
  • Disables Tracking and Location.
  • Cache and Cookies Cleared & Disabled.

Installation Instructions (PWA Only)

Follow these steps to install Portalocalhost as a Progressive Web App (PWA) on your device:

  1. Ensure you have a compatible browser (e.g., Chrome, Firefox, Edge).
  2. Navigate to the Portalocalhost website.
  3. In the browser menu, look for the option to "Install Portalocalhost" or "Add to Home screen".
  4. Follow the prompts to complete the installation.
  5. Once installed, you can access Portalocalhost from your home screen or app drawer.
